Research




From Online Broadcasting to the Survival of Urban Youth: An Attempt to Rebuild the Community

  • Discovered a micro process of a certain type of Online Broadcasting room by advancing the research methods, in order to analyze the assumption behavior and masculine reconstruction. Developed suggestions to address the social problems it causes.
  • Analyzed from a macro perspective where and why people use Online Broadcasting forums by scrawling big data of the geographical location of 10000+ online hosts and fans of YYlive, and compared the findings with the macroeconomic socio-economic data of provinces in China from the past 10 years from the National Bureau of Statistics.
  • Studied how online hosts and fans interact online by developing participatory observation techniques and online ethnography.


Discipline and Anti-Discipline - The Socialization Process of Nurses

  • Studied the conformity of stress caused by humanitarian care and nursing training.
  • Advanced participatory observation in three top hospitals (oncology, anorectal, emergency department) in Guangzhou for 15 days, worked with nurses and interviewed 5 nurses of varying experience levels.
