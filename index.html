Tianyao Qu

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Sociology at Cornell University. My research focuses on social networks, health, aging, and the digital economy, focusing on the Chinese affective live-stream industry. I research the dynamic relationship between social networks and physical and mental health, particularly among older adults, and the health implications for their social networks. I also study how people engage in the growing live-streaming industry in China (e.g., virtual gifting) with respect to the broader social structure.



Research Interests

Social Networks, Health, Aging and the Life Course, Economic Sociology, Communication Technology, Gender and Sexuality
